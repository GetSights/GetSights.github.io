What is GetSights?

GetSights is free mobile app, where you can find and share interesting sights, lovely places or great locations with each other.

Each photo has a good quality, category and geolocation so you can get to this lovely place by car, bicycle, electric scooter or by feet. If you know that some photo has incorrect geolocation you can make a report and send correct geolocation as well.

With GetSights you can planning your trip or regular walk, create, save and share your routes with your friends. Also you can use routes which have been created by travel bloggers, professional travellers or other users.

With GetSights you can do:

  • find the best sightseeing, lovely places or great locations around the world
  • draw you own polygon for searching sights
  • see only good quality content, because each photo will be reviewed by our moderators
  • create your own routes from liked photos
  • open routes on Google Map (Navigator)
  • save your routes
  • share your routes with other people
  • mark your routes like a private or public
  • add link to your instagram profile to each photo uploaded by you
  • find routes from travel bloggers, professional travellers or other users
  • etc...

If you would like to send feedback, improve GetSights or support us please feel free to send your email to getsights.info@gmail.com

Enjoy your trip!
